# Privacy Policy

**p10trancer** does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal
data. Full stop.

This policy applies to the **p10trancer** iPad application
("the app").

## What we don't do

- We do not collect personally identifiable information.
- We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or
  IDFA.
- We do not run analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting against any
  remote service.
- We do not include third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or social-
  login providers.
- We do not display ads.
- We do not require an account, login, or registration.

## What the app accesses on your device

- **Camera and microphone:** only when you assign a camera as a source
  for one of the app's pads. The video and audio feed is rendered
  locally and is not transmitted off the device. The camera and
  microphone are released as soon as that source is replaced.
- **Local network (MIDI):** the app uses CoreMIDI's Network MIDI
  feature to send and receive MIDI events to and from other devices
  on the same local network. This is local-network communication
  only. No data is sent to any remote server.
- **Files in the app's Documents folder:** loaded videos, mix
  recordings, saved sessions, and saved automation takes are written
  to the app's sandbox in `~/Documents/`. You can read, copy, and
  delete these via the iPad's Files application. They never leave
  your device unless you share them yourself.

## Data retention

All app-generated data lives in the app's sandbox and is removed when
you delete the app. You can also delete content earlier via the iPad's
Files app or the in-app session and recording management.

## Children

The app is not directed at children. It collects no data, so it
collects no data from children either.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be committed to the
project repository at the URL above. The change history is public via
the repository's git history.
